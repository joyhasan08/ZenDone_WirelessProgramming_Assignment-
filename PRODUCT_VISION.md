# ZenDone AI: Product Vision & Documentation

## 1. Executive Summary

**ZenDone** is not just a to-do list; it is a focus-oriented productivity partner. By combining a minimalist "Zen" aesthetic with the cognitive power of the **Gemini AI**, ZenDone transforms a static list of chores into a dynamic, encouraging journey toward goal completion.

---

## 2. Core Functionality

ZenDone provides a frictionless interface for task management with the following key features:

- **Smart AI Motivation**: Utilizing `gemini-3-flash-preview`, the app analyzes your current progress (tasks completed vs. total) and provides a unique, context-aware motivational tip.
- **Persistence-First Design**: Tasks are automatically synced to local storage, ensuring no data loss without requiring a complex login or database.
- **State-Driven Filtering**: A responsive navigation system allows users to pivot between "Pending" focus and "Completed" satisfaction.
- **Zero-Distraction UI**: Designed with Tailwind CSS, utilizing soft shadows, rounded "Squircle" corners, and a calming color palette to reduce digital anxiety.

## 4. Target Audience

Who will benefit most from ZenDone?

- **Students & Academics**: Managing multiple deadlines while needing consistent encouragement during long study sessions.
- **Creative Professionals**: Those who need a "brain dump" for ideas but get overwhelmed by cluttered interfaces.
- **Neurodivergent Individuals (ADHD/Executive Dysfunction)**: The minimalist layout and AI-driven positive reinforcement help maintain focus and reduce the "wall of awful" associated with starting tasks.
- **Minimalist Enthusiasts**: People who follow "Getting Things Done" (GTD) principles and want a tool that stays out of their way.

---

## 5. Future Evolution & Roadmap

ZenDone is designed to grow into a fully autonomous productivity agent.

### Phase 1: Enhanced Interaction (Short-term)

- **Voice-to-Task**: Integration of Gemini Native Audio to allow users to "speak" tasks into existence while on the go.
- **Task Categorization**: Smart tags (e.g., #Work, #Personal, #Urgent) that color-code the UI dynamically.

### Phase 2: Visual & Contextual Intelligence (Mid-term)

- **AI Image Generation**: Using `gemini-2.5-flash-image` to generate "Vision Board" images for high-priority goals, turning a text task into a visual inspiration.
- **Smart Scheduling**: AI that suggests the best time to perform a task based on the user's current workload.

### Phase 3: Ecosystem & Integration (Long-term)

- **Cross-Device Syncing**: Implementation of a lightweight backend for multi-device support.
- **Focus Soundscapes**: AI-generated ambient music that adjusts its intensity based on how many "Pending" tasks remain.
